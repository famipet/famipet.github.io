ABOUT US

Welcome to FAMIPET

A B2B cross-border e-commerce platform for pet products.

Corporate vision and Mission: Our vision is to become the preferred B2B platform for the global pet products industry, and to strengthen the connection between China factories and global enterprises by promoting international trade. Our mission is to use professional supply chain management and market insight to provide partners with unparalleled purchasing experience and improve product competitiveness and profits. At the same time, promote the global competitiveness of China’s pet products manufacturing industry.

Products and services: FAMIPET provides a series of pet products, covering pet toys, clothing, daily necessities and other categories. We work closely with the factory in China after strict screening to ensure that all products meet international standards and quality certification. Through our platform, buyers can easily find the products they need and enjoy competitive prices and efficient logistics services.

Supply chain advantages: Our core competitiveness lies in our strong supply chain management ability. We have established solid cooperative relations with many factories in China, and can provide customized production services and flexible order requirements. In addition, our logistics team has rich experience in international trade and can ensure that products are delivered to customers all over the world safely and in time.

Customer care and support: We know that the needs of customers are the core of our work. Our sales and customer service team consists of people with many years of experience in pet rearing. And very familiar with the pet market. They can provide professional product consultation and market trend analysis. We promise to provide our customers with high quality and low price products to help them expand their business and enhance their market competitiveness.

Social responsibility and cooperation: As a socially responsible enterprise, we are committed to promoting the sustainable development of the pet industry. We encourage factories to adopt environmentally friendly materials and production processes, and actively participate in public welfare activities to support pet welfare and protection projects. We believe that by working together with partners in the global pet products industry, we can create a better future for pets and pet owners.

We sincerely invite distributors and retailers of pet products all over the world to join FAMIPET, and jointly explore broad market prospects with us. Thank you for your trust and support. Let’s join hands to create a brilliant new chapter in pet products trade!
